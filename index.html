What are Tinkerhub chapters

Tinkerhub chapters are the products of Tinkerhub campus expansion project to build a peer to peer technology learning culture in campuses.

Why building Tinkerhub chapters

Fourth industrial revolution is coming and it is all about knowledge and skills. Our campuses need a community ecosystem that focus on peer learning. Read the experience shared by peers from our RIT chapter.

Get Started

From the experience of our previous campus initiatives we created a recipe for you to start an awesome TH chapter in your campus. We are looking forward to taste the additional ingredients you are going to add for taking the initiative to further levels.

Step 2

Tinkerhub is a people first community. The goal of Tinkerhub in campus is to help you, our fellow campus community member to learn 21st century technology and grab the opportunities provided by the same. Every one of you. We are not recommending any hierarchical structure for leading the campus community.

Even though, for every community there will be a set of founders, leads who trigger the vision. Second step is to find your team. Here are some guidelines for finding the team.

  • Find people with interest in putting effort to learn of technology, share knowledge and help others.
  • Talk to them about Tinkerhub, how building a chapter will make an impact, what you are planning to do.
  • Listen to their vision and ensure that this align with yours.
  • People who are already a member of a lot of communities are not encouraged to be in the lead team. Leads should be able to spend reasonable time in conducting Tinkerhub chapter operations.
  • Recognise and avoid people who join the community just for reputation or filing their resume.
